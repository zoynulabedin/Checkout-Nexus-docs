## How to Replace Placeholders

### Step 1: Upload Screenshots to Shopify

1. Go to your Shopify Partners Dashboard
2. Navigate to your app listing
3. Upload the 19 screenshots to the app store listing
4. Shopify will generate CDN URLs for each image

### Step 2: Get CDN URLs

The CDN URLs will follow this format:

```
https://cdn.shopify.com/app-store/listing_images/{IMAGE_ID}/desktop_screenshot/{filename}.png
```

### Step 3: Replace in docs-page.html

Find all instances of `YOUR_IMAGE_ID` in docs-page.html and replace with actual Shopify CDN URLs.

### Quick Find & Replace Command:

```bash
# On Windows (PowerShell)
(Get-Content docs-page.html) -replace 'YOUR_IMAGE_ID', 'actual-image-id' | Set-Content docs-page.html
```
